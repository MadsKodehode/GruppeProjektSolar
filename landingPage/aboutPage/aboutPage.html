About Us

This project has been created by two guys in Bergen in a cold October week. It is designed to be an aid in teaching kids about our solar system as part of the early education. It has a simple design that's easy to understand, and it contains playful images and icons and bite-sized facts that serve as an entry to learning more about what's out there in the big universe. We hope you enjoy our creation, feel free to let us know what you think by using the form in the "contact us" section.
As always, fly safe and remember your space suit!
